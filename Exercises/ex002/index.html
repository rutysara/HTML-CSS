Dreams & Ideals

  As we embark on our youthful journeys and envision our perfect destinies, the stark truths of existence often catch us off guard. Our aspirations and ideals, unfortunately, frequently dissipate without materializing. This brings about a rush of anxiety, sadness, frustration, and anger within us. Nevertheless, as we mature and gain wisdom through life's experiences, we gradually let go and forge ahead.
Yet, I firmly believe that, to a certain degree, we possess the power to actualize our dreams and ideals.
