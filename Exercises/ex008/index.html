Hosting External Videos

Adding videos from external servers, like Youtube, it is super simple:

  1. Choose the video you want
  2. Click share and choose the embed option
  3. Copy and paste code in your site

Upload your video to your own Youtube channel and then add on your site. This will mean the data traffic for the video will be handled by Youtube and not your site.

You can also upload to Vimeo instead of Youtube.

Advantages:

  • Better quality.
  • More personalised options available - if you are building an online school, for example, you want your videos to be only for website private members.
  • No suggested videos of different channels appear.
Disadvantages:
  • Paid membership.
  • Lower data traffic due to inefficient algorithms.
  • Fast and Good Internet connection needed for fluidity of video.
