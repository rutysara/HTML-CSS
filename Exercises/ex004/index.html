More Text Formatting

BIG and SMALL

I can use BIG or SMALL to increase or decrease letters and words.
However, BIG has no meaning besides styling - in this case CSS should be used - so it became or will be an obsolete tag and will likely stop working any time soon.
SMALL on the other hand has semantic, in a contract some times they have a warning of small reading at the bottom or something that are important to read. So, using the SMALL tag has meaning in HTML.

INSERT and DELETE

I can use DEL to cross over words that I want to show as crossed!
It is really fun to do this!
I can use INS to underline words as I see fit.
